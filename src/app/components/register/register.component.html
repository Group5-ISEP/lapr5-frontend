How will we use your data?

PORTOBUS collects your data so that we can:

How do we store your data?

PORTOBUS securely stores your data at the company Data Center systems.

PORTOBUS will keep your personal identification information for 1 year starting after the account registration or renewal of consent. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by deleting every reference in our Data Center records. This means you will no longer be able to access our services. If you wish to continue using them, please read the email that will be sent when 1 month is left for consent to expire. Otherwise, you will have to create a new account.

What are your data protection rights?

PORTOBUS would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request PORTOBUS for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that PORTOBUS correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request PORTOBUS to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that PORTOBUS erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that PORTOBUS restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to PORTOBUS’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that PORTOBUS transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: 1180622@isep.ipp.pt
